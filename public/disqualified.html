Unfortunately, at this time your property doesn't qualify for this scheme.

This can be for a variety of reasons, including:

  • Being connected to the mains gas supply;
  • Not being connected to the mains electricity supply;
  • Having existing solar panels (this would usually mean that the starting EPC rating is too high, and prevent the application of additional panels, which is mandatory under the scheme);
  • Having a listed home. Unfortunately, we are not currently accepting applications from listed buildings unless full planning permission is already in place for the list of grant measures. If this is the case for you, please email clientservices@ruralenergysavinggrants.org and we will see if we can assist.
  • Not having a qualifying applicant in your household, i.e. someone with a qualifying medical condition, benefit, or a combined household income of under £31,000 per year.

We understand this might be disappointing. However, eligibility criteria for these grants can change over time, and you might become eligible in the future.

The good news is, the qualification criteria for this grant will likely be opening up to more people...
