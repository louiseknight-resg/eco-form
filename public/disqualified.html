Unfortunately, your property doesn't currently qualify for this scheme.

This can be for a variety of reasons, including:

  • Your EPC (Energy Performance Certificate) is too high. Many websites will assert that homes rated D-G will qualify for heating and solar grants, however, since the November 2025 budget release, funding rates have reduced significantly. Subsequently, only F/G rated homes are qualifying for air source and solar PV funding.
  • Presently, homes rated D-G can only qualify for solid wall insulation only under very specific criteria via a different funding route. We automatically review your home upon application for all possible funding routes.
  • Having existing solar panels (this would usually mean that the starting EPC rating is too high, and prevent the application of additional panels, which is mandatory under the primary scheme)
  • Not having a qualifying applicant in your household, i.e. someone with a qualifying medical condition, benefit, or a combined household income of under £31,000 per year

We understand this might be disappointing. However, we are expecting the next iteration of the grant to commence this year, pending further details.
